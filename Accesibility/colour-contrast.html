Color Contrast & Accessibility

WCAG Contrast Ratio Requirements

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define minimum contrast ratios between text and its background:

  • Level AA (Normal text): Minimum ratio of 4.5:1
  • Level AA (Large text ≥18pt or 14pt bold): Minimum ratio of 3:1
  • Level AAA (Normal text): Minimum ratio of 7:1
  • Level AAA (Large text): Minimum ratio of 4.5:1

How Does It Work?

Contrast ratio is calculated using the relative luminance of two colors:

  • Each color's RGB values are converted to linear light values
  • Relative luminance (L) is computed: L = 0.2126*R + 0.7152*G + 0.0722*B
  • Contrast ratio = (L1 + 0.05) / (L2 + 0.05) where L1 is the lighter color
  • The ratio ranges from 1:1 (no contrast) to 21:1 (black on white)
